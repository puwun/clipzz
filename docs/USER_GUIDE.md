Complete guide to using Clipzz for creating viral-ready podcast clips.

## <img src="https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/latest/1f44b/512.gif" width="24"> Welcome to Clipzz!

Clipzz automatically transforms your long-form podcast videos into engaging short-form vertical clips perfect for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts. Our AI identifies the most engaging moments, tracks active speakers, and adds beautiful karaoke-style subtitles.

---

## <img src="https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/latest/1f680/512.gif" width="24"> Getting Started

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit [clipzzz.vercel.app](https://clipzzz.vercel.app)
2. Click **"Sign Up"**
3. Choose your preferred method:
   - **Email & Password** - Create credentials
   - **Google Sign-In** - Quick OAuth login
4. **Receive 10 free credits** to test the service!

**What are credits?**
- 1 credit = 1 generated clip
- Credits never expire
- Purchase more credits anytime

---

### Step 2: Upload Your Podcast

1. **Navigate to Dashboard** after logging in
2. **Drag and drop** your video file or click to browse
3. **Supported formats:**
   - MP4 (recommended)
   - AVI
   - MOV
4. **File requirements:**
   - Video should feature visible speakers
   - Audio should be clear (podcast/interview style)
   - Recommended: 10-60 minute videos
   - Works best with Q&A format content

**<img src="https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/latest/1f4a1/512.gif" width="16"> Pro Tip:** Longer videos (30-60 min) typically yield more high-quality clips.

---

### Step 3: Choose Number of Clips

1. **Select how many clips** you want (1-10+)
2. **Check your credits:**
   - Each clip costs 1 credit
   - Requesting 3 clips = 3 credits
3. **Click "Process Video"**

**What happens next?**
- Video uploads to secure cloud storage
- AI transcribes your audio (3-5 minutes)
- Gemini AI identifies engaging moments
- Clips are generated with subtitles (2-3 minutes per clip)

---

### Step 4: Monitor Processing

Your upload appears in the **"Queue"** table with status:

| Status | Meaning | What to Do |
|--------|---------|------------|
| <img src="https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/latest/23f3/512.gif" width="16"> **Queued** | Waiting to process | Wait for processing to start |
| <img src="https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/latest/2699/512.gif" width="16"> **Processing** | AI is working on your video | Check back in 10-15 minutes |
| <img src="https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/latest/2705/512.gif" width="16"> **Processed** | Clips ready! | View in "My Clips" tab |
| <img src="https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/latest/1f4b3/512.gif" width="16"> **No Credits** | Insufficient credits | Purchase credits to continue |
| <img src="https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/latest/274c/512.gif" width="16"> **Failed** | Error occurred | Contact support |

**Typical processing time:**
- 1-hour podcast, 3 clips: ~12-15 minutes
- Shorter videos process faster

**<img src="https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/latest/2615/512.gif" width="16"> Note:** Processing is async. You can close the browser and return later!

---

### Step 5: View Your Clips

1. **Click "My Clips" tab** in dashboard
2. **Browse your generated clips** in gallery view
3. **Each clip shows:**
   - Thumbnail preview
   - Duration (30-60 seconds)
   - Creation date
4. **Click any clip** to play in browser

---

### Step 6: Download and Share

1. **Click the download button** on any clip
2. **Video specifications:**
   - Resolution: 1080x1920 (vertical)
   - Format: MP4 (H.264)
   - Audio: AAC, 128kbps
   - Subtitles: Burned-in (cannot be removed)
3. **Upload to social media:**
   - TikTok
   - Instagram Reels
   - YouTube Shorts
   - Facebook Reels
   - LinkedIn

**<img src="https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/latest/1f4f1/512.gif" width="16"> Perfect for Mobile:** All clips are optimized for vertical mobile viewing!

- **Identifies engaging moments:**

  - Interesting stories

### <img src="https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/latest/1f441/512.gif" width="20"> Active Speaker Detection

- **LR-ASD model** tracks faces in your video
- **Identifies who's speaking** frame-by-frame
- **Smart cropping:**
  - Camera follows active speaker
  - Centered on speaker's face
  - Smooth tracking (no jitter)
- **Fallback:** Blurred letterbox when no speaker detected

**Result:** Professional-looking clips that focus on the person talking.

### <img src="https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/latest/1f3a5/512.gif" width="20"> Video Quality Tips

**For best results:**
1. **Clear audio** - Minimize background noise
2. **Visible faces** - Camera should capture speakers
3. **Good lighting** - Well-lit subjects
4. **Stable camera** - Avoid excessive movement
5. **Q&A format** - Questions and answers work best

**<img src="https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/latest/26a0/512.gif" width="16"> Avoid:**
- Pure monologues (no interaction)
- Music-heavy content
- Low-quality phone recordings
- Videos without visible speakers

## <img src="https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/latest/2753/512.gif" width="24"> Troubleshooting

### <img src="https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/latest/274c/512.gif" width="20"> No Clips Generated

**Problem:** Processing completes but 0 clips created.

**Causes:**
- Video doesn't have Q&A format content
- No clear questions and answers
- Video is too short (<5 minutes)
- AI couldn't identify engaging moments

**Solutions:**
- Try a different video with more dialogue
- Ensure video has question/answer discussions
- Upload longer content (20+ minutes)
- Contact support if issue persists

---

### <img src="https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/latest/23f3/512.gif" width="20"> Processing Stuck

**Problem:** Status stays on "Processing" for >30 minutes.

**Solutions:**
1. **Refresh the page** - Processing continues in background
2. **Check credits** - Ensure you have enough
3. **Wait longer** - Very long videos may take 20-30 minutes
4. **Contact support** - Provide upload ID

---

### <img src="https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/latest/1f4b3/512.gif" width="20"> Credits Not Added After Payment

**Problem:** Paid for credits but balance didn't update.

**Solutions:**
1. **Refresh the page**
2. **Check email** for payment confirmation
3. **Wait 5 minutes** for webhook processing
4. **Contact support** with:
   - Payment receipt
   - Transaction ID
   - Email used

**<img src="https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/latest/1f512/512.gif" width="16"> Note:** Credits usually add within 30 seconds of successful payment.

---

### <img src="https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/latest/1f507/512.gif" width="20"> Can't Play Clip

**Problem:** Clip won't load or play.

**Solutions:**
1. **Refresh the page** - Regenerates playback URL
2. **Try different browser**
3. **Check internet connection**
4. **Download instead** and play locally

---

### <img src="https://fonts.gstatic.com/s/e/notoemoji/latest/1f4e4/512.gif" width="20"> Upload Fails

**Problem:** Video won't upload.

**Causes:**
- File too large
- Unsupported format
- Network issues

**Solutions:**
1. **Check file size** - Try files under 2GB first
2. **Check format** - Use MP4 if possible
3. **Check internet** - Stable connection required
4. **Try again** - Transient network errors common
